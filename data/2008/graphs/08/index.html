Tera Capital Fund was launched in April 2005 as a fund of funds focused primarily on Russia. It was the first and remains the only fund of funds in the world with its specific geographic focus. It is also singular in that Altima Asset Management, the investment management company that manages Tera, charges no fees other than a success fee and also covers all of Tera's expenses.

Since inception, Altima has guided Tera to net returns to investors exceeding 35% annually. Tera has experienced positive growth for 12 out of the 13 quarters of its existence. While growing at this pace, Tera has proven to be a particularly strong defensive investment.

Among the more than 2,500 funds of funds worldwide ranked by Eurekahedge and out of the 38 months that Tera has been ranked by that company since inception, Tera has been ranked No. 1 in monthly performance three times, in the top 10 seven times, was ranked the best-performing fund of funds in the world for all of 2005, has been ranked No. 1 in performance YTD six times, has been ranked in the top 10 thirteen times, has been ranked No. 1 in terms of annualized performance nine times, has been ranked in the top 10 thirty six times (that is, all but one month of its existence) and, as of July 2008, was ranked No. 2 globally.

During every general market downturn of more than 10% in Russia since inception, Tera has fallen by a small fraction of the total market and of other funds: generally experiencing only 25-30% of the overall percentage decrease. This has given it a Sharpe Ratio that is generally 50% higher than that of the leading Russian indices and 35% higher than the average of other funds.
