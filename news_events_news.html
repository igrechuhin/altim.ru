October 2010. Hedge Funds Review, the leading alternative investment fund publication in Europe, nominated Tera Capital Fund as one of the three finalists for its award as the "Most Innovative Fund of Funds in Europe" for the period July 2009-Jun 2010.

October 2010. Tera Capital Fund is designated as one of three finalists for the award as the "Most Innovative Fund of Funds" in Europe for the period July 2009-June 2010 by Hedge Funds Review.

September 2010. Eurekahedge, which tracks 23,085 investment funds worldwide, including 2,433 funds of funds, released its "Top Ten" lists for April and ranked Tera Capital Fund as the number 1 fund of funds in the world in terms of 2010 performance YTD.

April 2010. Eurekahedge, which tracks 21,888 investment funds worldwide, including 2,336 funds of funds, released its "Top Ten" lists for April and ranked Tera Capital Fund as the number 2 fund of funds in the world in terms of 2010 performance YTD.

April 2010. Tera Capital Fund registered as a "tax transparent" fund in Germany.

March 2010. Eurekahedge, which tracks 21,888 investment funds worldwide, including 2,336 funds of funds, released its "Top Ten" lists for March and ranked Tera Capital Fund as the number 1 fund of funds in the world in terms of performance for March 2010 and number 2 in the world in terms of Q1 2010 performance.

March 2010. First institutional investors invest in Tera Capital Fund as a result of the February 2010 road show. First German investor invests in TCF and Altima Asset Management begins the process to ensure that Tera will be treated as a "transparent" fund for tax purposes in Germany.

March 15-16, 2010. Altima Asset Management conducts a road show and numerous one-on-one meetings in Paris, Geneva and Lyon.

February 22-26, 2010. Altima Asset Management conducts a "road show" for potential investors in Tera Capital Fund in Zurich, Munich, Stuttgart, Frankfurt, Amsterdam, Luxembourg City and Brussels.

January 2010. First attendee investor invests in Tera Capital Fund as a result of the road show held in Geneva and France in late November 2009.

December 31, 2009. Tera Capital Fund is ranked by Eurekahedge number 8 in terms of annualized return out of 2,344 fund of funds worldwide.

November 2009. Altima Asset Management conducts a "road show" for potential investors in Tera Capital Fund in Geneva and France.

September 8-12, 2008. Altima Asset Management conducted its first "road show" for potential investors into Tera Capital Fund. The Fund managers were in Zurich (September 8), Geneva (September 9), Paris (September 10), Luxembourg City and Brussels (September 11) and London (September 12).

March 30, 2008. Tera Capital Fund completed the third full year of operations. During those three years, it generated a total return of over 150% and an average annual rate of return of almost 40%, with a Sharpe Ratio of over 2. Its assets increased by 2,500%.

January 31, 2006. Tera Capital Fund is again ranked by Eurekahedge as the fund of funds in the world with the highest monthly performance (out of 2,600 funds of funds).

September 30, 2005. For the first time, Tera Capital Fund is ranked by Eurekahedge as the fund of funds with the highest annualized rate of return in the world (out of 2,600 funds of funds).

December 31, 2005. Tera Capital Fund completed 2005 ranked by Eurekahedge as the fund of funds in the world with the highest year-to-date performance (out of 2,600 funds of funds).
